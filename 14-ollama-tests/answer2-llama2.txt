Then, when a computer wants to find something specific, like a picture of a dog, it can look at all of those tiny pictures in the "book" and find the one it's looking for right away!

In generative AI, embeddings are used to help computers generate new things that are similar to things they have seen before. So, if a computer wants to generate a new picture of a dog, it can look at all of the tiny pictures in its "book" of ideas and combine them in new ways to create something fresh and exciting!
